Hi, I'm Naeem!

I'm currently a second year student working towards a degree in Computer Science. My journey with the Scandinavian Nordic Student Association (SNSA) has been brief, but undoubtedly memorable!

Getting to know those involved (as members, volunteers, executives, and faculty supporters) has demonstrated to me that the SNSA is something special. It is a collective of amazing people, who are kind, friendly, supportive, and welcoming. There is not a doubt in my mind that the club is an integral part of many lives, mine included.

I believe it's imperative to continue to help foster the sense of community that has grown amongst us. To continue to spread and celebrate the Scandinavian cultures and languages with you all. And to continue our journeys together, trying new things, hosting shared experiences, and learning evermore.

The SNSA is the best club at UBC; I plan to continue the incredible work set forth by the current executive team which has made it as such.

I am endorsed by these lovely people:

  • Ikra Aziz, SNSA VP Communications Candidate
  • Delaney Westby, Outgoing SNSA VP Community Coordinator and SNSA Presidential Candidate

My plans & goals for the club include:

  • Creating a stylish & easy to use website that represents the SNSA, adding an integral piece to the club's communication efforts
  • Engaging in early planning to ensure all events get the spotlight they deserve and the preparation time they would benefit from
  • Increasing our member count and improving the current system of membership to provide all members with a more seamless experience
